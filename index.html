I'm Alvin Aurellius, an Assistant F&B Marketing Manager at Sheraton Bali Kuta Resort, part of the Marriott International family. I manage the full marketing ecosystem for three distinct outlets: Daily Social Bali, Bene Italian Kitchen, and &more by Sheraton.

My work spans social media strategy, digital campaigns, press & PR, KOL collaborations, TripAdvisor management, SevenRooms implementation, and event marketing — from intimate chef's table dinners to large-scale music festivals.

Outside the hotel, I'm building a content creator brand on Instagram (@alvn.rls), creating Reels about office life, self-development, and the grind — for Gen Z Indonesians who want to grow their careers without losing themselves.

Currently pursuing a Bachelor's in Business Management to sharpen the strategic side of everything I do.
